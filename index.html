Brendon Urie Was Almost a Part of the Greatest Showman

Summary
During a radio interview, the radio hostess and Brendon Urie discuss Panic! At the Disco's opportunity to be involved with the The Greatest Showman's film soundtrack. Urie expresses he and the band would have loved to have been a part of the project, but those responsible for the film score never got back to them. Urie goes on to express that he would still love to do a cover and/or mashup of The Greatest Showman songs in the future. Top
